About Us

Meet the Maker

Yvonne Caole-Sousa launched UME in 2015 after years of management in the hospitality industry. Her career was launched while finishing her degree in Sports, Entertainment and Event Management at the University of Johnson & Wales. Following, she gained knowledge and experienced throughout her trade as a Banquet Manager, Food and Beverage Director and Social Catering Sales Manager for a Luxury Boutique hotel and a Golf Resort. Soon she realized working for a venue was limited and she craved more. With the support of past clients, UME was launched and has been growing ever since!

Creating Memorable Events

With over ten years of Event Planning in Worcester and New England, we know how to make your event one to remember. From the decorations to the entertainment, we help you build your event from the ground up. Check out the gallery to see some memories from our past events from Wedding Planning to Day of Coordination.

Versatile Service

Our wide range of vendors ensure you have exactly the event you want. From corporate event functions, Weddings and Birthday parties, we help you find the event professionals that works for your style and budget. Contact us to meet with our planner and talk about your many options.
